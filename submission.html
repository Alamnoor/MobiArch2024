MobiArch 2024

MobiArch 2024: Workshop on Mobility in the Evolving Internet Architecture; Proposal track: research-track workshop with ACM MobiCom 2024

Washington, D.C., USA, 18th November, 2024

Program Format

We plan to continue our tradition of MobiArch workshops with a one-day single-track program. Apart from the traditional full-paper presentations, we will include more interactive forums, including short and position papers, keynote speeches, and panel/round table discussions, to foster discussions and attendee involvement. The strategy of organizing keynotes and panel discussions has been demonstrated very effectively in our past MobiArch workshops.

Publicity Plan

We will set up a workshop website and post Call for Paper messages on the social media such as Facebook, Twitter, and Linkedin, and regularly publicize the Call for Paper emails via the research community email list such as SIGMOBILE-members, SIGCOMM-members, TCCC, ITC, TCCN, WTC, IoT, and TCBD.
